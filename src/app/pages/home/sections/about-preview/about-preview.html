About NTG Excavation

With over 40 years of experience, NTG Excavation is a Gozo-based company specialising in excavation, demolition, rock cutting, and skip services. Trusted for its professionalism and reliability, the company has successfully delivered projects across Malta and Gozo, from residential works to large scale industrial sites.

Backed by modern machinery, an experienced team, and industry leading facilities, NTG Excavation is equipped to handle projects of any scale - efficiently, safely, and on time.
