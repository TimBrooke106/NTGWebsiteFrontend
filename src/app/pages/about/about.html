About NTG Excavations

Three decades of excellence in construction services

31 Years of Trusted Service

With origins dating back over 40 years, NTG Excavation is a Gozo based company specialising in excavation, demolition, rock cutting works and skip service. The experience accumulated over these decades forms the backbone of the professional and high quality services for which NTG Excavation is known. Over the years, the company has successfully handled a wide variety of projects across Malta and Gozo. NTG Excavation has the skills, expertise, and equipment required to manage and execute projects ranging from small residential works to large industrial sites that demand formal planning, coordination, and operational management. The company is fully equipped with its own fleet of trucks and modern machinery, tools, and equipment capable of handling works of varying scale and complexity. Its extensive range of earth moving machinery including excavators, bulldozers, trench cutters, and mini excavators which makes NTG Excavation one of the most well equipped companies in the sector, enabling projects to be completed in the most efficient manner and shortest possible time. NTG Excavation is also the only company in Gozo that operates its own fully permitted and legal landfill. This facility consists of a former quarry that has been converted into a dumping site, complete with certified weighing equipment.

Our journey has been built on a foundation of hard work, dedication, and an unwavering commitment to our clients. Over the past three decades, we've completed thousands of projects, each one reinforcing our reputation for quality, reliability, and professionalism.
